Ada's Engine

Inspiring women in computer science

Why?

     I'm a second year computer science student at the University of Waterloo. As I survey the sea of faces in my classes, something is amiss. Women are highly underrepresented in the field of computer science. Ada's Engine will be one solution for this issue.

     Ada's Engine is a start-up dedicated to encouraging young women to pursue an interest in computer science and other STEM fields. Named after Ada Lovelace, the world's first computer programmer, and her work with the Analytical Engine, we hope to inspire women to pursue passions they never knew they had, develop skills they never knew they were capable of, and accomplish things they never dreamt were possible. 

What we do

We are dedicated to inspiring young women to pursue their passions regardless of social barriers. We have online tutorials to teach Python, a common scripting language, and we are organising workshops to provide hands-on experience.
